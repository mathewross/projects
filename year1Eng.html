    Luxury Camping Grill Tool
    Date completed: May 2017 (first year project)


    Brief: To create a device that contained some form of opening and locking mechanism


    The object that I have designed has adhered to a specification that specifically required me to develop a small compatible/expandable/modular product.

    It had to have a number of components that interacted with each other, product should collapse, expand, extend to offer at least 2 states of operation, at least one mechanism/mechanical action, needed to be constructed of no less than 3 materials.

    The result is a handheld camping grilling device that can be used to cook a variety of meats and fish and is flexible to use. The product is designed to cook food such as chicken and fish on top of a campfire and is to be handheld for briefly applying heat to food.
