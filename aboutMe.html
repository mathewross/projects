    Who am I?


    I am Charlotte Mears, an Industrial Design student at University of Liverpool, currently in my second year of study.


    How did I get here?


    Originally from Liverpool United Kingdom, I have always been a creative. Originally I was an artist having studied the subject at Liverpool City College and University of Reading.

    That was until I was inspired to change, it was a trip to the Design Museum in London that triggered an interest that had been in me without even knowing it. I made the switch to become a Design Engineer.

    What am I up to?


    I am currently in the middle of my second year of study, where I am learning more about product development, human factors and management. I'm currently looking for a full year industrial placement for the forthcoming 2018 academic year.

    Hobbies

    - Running, completed Liverpool half marathon and Spring 10k
    - Visiting design museums such as Copenhagen, London and Oslo
    - Keeping up to date with latest tech, including experimenting with Arduino boards
    - Participated in ACT Cadets, which allowed me to experience flying an aircraft, shooting, cross country and completing the Duke of Edinburgh Bronze Award
    - Slacklining
    - Cycling
